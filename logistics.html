How I Handle Your Trade-In and Lease Equity

If you are currently driving a vehicle that you own or lease, it is highly likely we can leverage its value to offset your next purchase. Here is exactly how I structure that process for you under New York State regulations:

  • Positive Equity Math: If your vehicle is worth more than the remaining loan balance, I apply that positive equity directly to your new deal structure as a down payment, lowering your monthly cost.
  • The NY State Sales Tax Advantage: When you trade in a vehicle you own in New York, you only pay sales tax on the difference between your new vehicle’s price and your trade-in's value. I make sure this structural tax savings is clearly calculated right on your deal sheet.
  • Lease Returns: Even if your current lease isn't a GM vehicle, bring it to me. I handle the grounding logistics, inspect the condition with you, and look for equity opportunities to roll forward.

Your Delivery Day Checklist

To ensure your transition is seamless and your time is protected, I require a few critical physical items when you arrive for your delivery appointment.

  1. Your Physical Title or Lien Release: If you own your trade-in outright, I need the physical title. If you recently paid off a loan, bring the official Lien Release document from your bank.
  2. Current Registration & Insurance: New York State requires your active registration card to complete a legal plate transfer. I will coordinate directly with your insurance provider to switch your coverage on-site.
  3. All Keys and Remotes: To maximize the final valuation on your trade-in, ensure both sets of keys are present at the time of trade desking.

Seamless NY Plate Transfers

I handle all of your DMV processing on-site so you never have to stand in a line.

  • Transferring Existing Plates: If you want to keep your current Buffalo-market plates, I will process the transfer directly onto your new registration.
  • New Plate Issuance: If your current plates are worn, peeling, or you need a completely fresh registration, I issue official New York State permanent plates right from my desk at the time of delivery.
