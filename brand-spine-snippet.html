The Point-of-Sale EV Rebate Advantage

If you are evaluating an electric vehicle like the Equinox EV or Silverado EV, the financial math has shifted heavily in your favor. I make sure my clients maximize these incentives right at the time of deal desking, rather than waiting until tax season next year:

  • Federal EV Tax Credit: A $7,500 credit applied directly as a down payment reduction at the point of sale on qualified models.
  • New York State Drive Clean Rebate: Up to an additional $2,000 in regional state reductions depending on the vehicle's electric range, subtracted immediately from your total cost.
